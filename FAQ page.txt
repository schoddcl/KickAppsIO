1) How can I run the app?
	- Follow the README.md file and go through step by step
2) How can I add a Professor?
	- First make sure you have followed the README.md file.
	Then log in to your account, press the ADD button, fill out
	the fields you know, press submit, and finally wait for an admin
	to accept or deny your submission.
3) How can I comment?
	- Log in to your account and find the Professor you wish to comment on.
	Press the 'comments' button on the far right, type out the comment you
	wish to add, and press the 'comment' button.
